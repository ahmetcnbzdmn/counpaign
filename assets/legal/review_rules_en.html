Counpaign Review and Content Rules

Last updated: 17.03.2026

This document has been prepared to explain the rules under which user reviews and content submitted through the Counpaign mobile application may be created.

1. Purpose

Reviews made through Counpaign are collected to help partnered cafes improve their service quality and to provide feedback on user experience.

2. Visibility and Usage

  • Reviews are not publicly published to end users.
  • Reviews may only be shared with the relevant partnered business.
  • User information shown to the business may be limited to the user's first name and last name initial.

3. Content Rules

Users agree to comply with the following rules when creating reviews:

  • Reviews must be based on a real visit or experience.
  • Content containing insults, threats, profanity, degradation, hate speech, and discriminatory expressions must not be shared.
  • False, misleading, or defamatory content must not be created.
  • Personal data, contact information, or confidential information belonging to other individuals must not be shared.
  • Content contrary to law, public morality, and third-party rights must not be created.

4. Counpaign's Authority

  • Counpaign may remove, restrict the visibility of, or investigate content that it deems to be in violation of these rules.
  • When deemed necessary, the relevant user account may be temporarily suspended or terminated.
  • Necessary actions may be taken in accordance with requests from authorized authorities as required by legislation.

5. Responsibility

The User is personally responsible for the reviews and content they create. Counpaign reserves its legal rights regarding damages arising from the creation of content that is contrary to law or these rules.

6. Contact

For requests within the scope of this document: info@counpaign.com
