This Notice has been prepared in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK") to inform users of the Counpaign mobile application about the scope of personal data processing, to whom it may be transferred, the legal bases, and the rights of data subjects.

1. Data Controller

Your personal data is processed by Levent Canatar as the data controller within the scope described below.

2. Categories of Personal Data Processed

  • Identity Data: name, surname, date of birth, gender
  • Contact Data: phone number, email address
  • Transaction Data: membership records, verification records, point and stamp movements, usage history per partnered cafe
  • User Experience and Feedback Data: user reviews, ratings, feedback
  • Technical Data: application usage logs, error records, device information, session information, and security records

3. Purposes of Personal Data Processing

Your personal data may be processed for the following purposes:

  • creation and management of membership accounts,
  • conducting account verification processes,
  • operating the loyalty program,
  • managing point and stamp earning/usage processes,
  • tracking campaign eligibility and benefits on a per-partnered-cafe basis,
  • forwarding user reviews to the relevant business,
  • conducting support, request, and complaint processes,
  • ensuring service security, preventing and auditing misuse,
  • fulfilling obligations arising from legislation,
  • conducting processes necessary for the establishment, exercise, or protection of a right.

4. Parties to Whom Personal Data May Be Transferred and Transfer Purposes

Your personal data may be transferred to the following parties to the extent necessary and for the purposes stated above:

  • Partnered Cafes: For the purpose of operating loyalty programs, managing point/stamp processes, and forwarding user feedback to the relevant business.
  • Suppliers and Technical Service Providers: For the purpose of providing hosting, software infrastructure, technical support, verification, and security services.
  • Authorized Public Institutions and Organizations: Upon request under relevant legislation and limited to the purpose of the request.

User reviews are not shared publicly with end users. These reviews may only be transferred to the relevant business with limited visibility in the format of the user's first name and last name initial.

5. Method and Legal Basis of Personal Data Collection

Your personal data is collected through the Counpaign mobile application electronically, partially or fully through automated means.

Your personal data is processed based on the following legal grounds:

  • processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which the data subject is party,
  • processing is necessary for the data controller to fulfill its legal obligations,
  • processing is necessary for the establishment, exercise, or protection of a right,
  • processing is necessary for the legitimate interests of the data controller, provided that the fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject are not harmed,
  • explicit consent of the data subject where required.

6. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under Article 11 of KVKK, you have the following rights:

  • to learn whether your personal data is being processed,
  • to request information if your personal data has been processed,
  • to learn the purpose of processing your personal data and whether they are used in accordance with their purpose,
  • to know the third parties to whom your personal data is transferred domestically or abroad,
  • to request correction of personal data if it is incomplete or incorrectly processed,
  • to request deletion or destruction of personal data when the reasons requiring its processing no longer exist, even though it has been processed in accordance with KVKK and relevant legislation,
  • to request notification of correction, deletion, or destruction operations to third parties to whom personal data has been transferred,
  • to object to a result that is against you through analysis of processed data exclusively by automated systems,
  • to claim compensation for damages arising from unlawful processing of personal data.

7. Application Method

You may submit your requests within the scope of KVKK, together with information and documents verifying your identity, through the following contact information:

Email: info@counpaign.com

Your applications will be concluded within the shortest time possible and within thirty days at the latest within the framework of current legislation.
