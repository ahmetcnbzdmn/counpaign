1. Subject and Scope

The subject of this Agreement is to determine the procedures and principles regarding the User's participation in loyalty programs of partnered cafes through the Counpaign mobile application, earning and using points and/or stamps on a per-cafe basis, benefiting from campaigns, and submitting reviews about their visit experience.

Counpaign is not a payment institution, electronic money institution, or a platform that directly collects orders. Counpaign provides a mobile application infrastructure that helps partnered cafes digitally manage their loyalty and campaign processes.

2. Definitions

  • Counpaign: The digital platform that enables users to participate in loyalty programs of partnered cafes through the mobile application.
  • User: The individual who becomes a member of the Counpaign mobile application by accepting this Agreement.
  • Partnered Cafe: Independent businesses within the Counpaign system that create their own loyalty programs.
  • Points: Loyalty points earned and used within the rules set by the relevant partnered cafe.
  • Stamps: Digital stamps earned under conditions determined by the relevant partnered cafe, providing access to certain benefits.
  • Review: Opinions, ratings, and feedback submitted by the User through the application after a visit or experience.
  • Account: The membership record associated with the User's phone number and other membership information.

3. Membership and Account

  • Counpaign membership is free.
  • The User is obligated to provide accurate, up-to-date, and personal information when registering for the application.
  • The account is personal; it cannot be transferred, rented, or made available for use by third parties.
  • SMS verification codes may be sent for account verification and security purposes.
  • The User acknowledges that the phone number belongs to them and that verifications made through this number will constitute transactions related to their account.

4. Nature of Service

  • Counpaign offers users the ability to track loyalty programs of multiple partnered cafes through a single application.
  • Points and stamps are maintained separately for each partnered cafe, not as a shared balance.
  • Each partnered cafe can determine its own campaign, point, stamp, benefit, and usage conditions.
  • Counpaign ensures the digital display and implementation of the rules defined by the relevant cafe.

5. Point and Stamp Rules

  • The earning, usage, validity, and expiration conditions of points and stamps may vary on a per-partnered-cafe basis.
  • Points and stamps cannot be converted to cash, transferred to another user, or claimed in lieu of money.
  • The User can only earn points and stamps from their own transactions.
  • Points and stamps related to cancelled, invalidated, erroneously created, or suspected misuse transactions may be reversed or cancelled.
  • The User agrees not to attempt to obtain points or stamps by exploiting system vulnerabilities or creating misleading transactions.

6. Reviews and Feedback

  • The User can submit reviews about the partnered cafe they visited through the application.
  • These reviews are not publicly visible to end users.
  • Reviews may only be shown to the relevant business with limited visibility in the format of the user's first name and last name initial.
  • The User agrees not to share content containing insults, threats, profanity, degrading expressions, false statements, personal data disclosure, or content that violates third-party rights.
  • Counpaign reserves the right to remove, restrict the visibility of, or investigate reviews that are contrary to law, public morality, or this Agreement.
